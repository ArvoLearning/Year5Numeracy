Bulk Buying Scenario

Calculate cost per store, then decide the best option

⏱ 20 mins

You need to buy 30 packets of sticky notes for a project. Three stores offer different deals. Calculate the total cost from each store, then choose the best option.

Store A

Individual packets
$1.50 each
Need: 30 packets
30 × $1.50 = ?
$45.00

Store B

Box of 10 packets
$12.00 per box
Need 3 boxes (30 ÷ 10)
3 × $12.00 = ?
$36.00

Store C

Box of 25 packets
$30.00 per box
Need 2 boxes → 50 packets
2 × $30.00 = ?
$60.00

🏆 Store B is the cheapest at $36.00

Store A: $45.00 (30 × $1.50) — convenient but most expensive
Store B: $36.00 (3 × $12.00) — cheapest for exactly 30
Store C: $60.00 (2 × $30.00) — cheapest per packet ($1.20 each), but you buy 50 and only need 30 — wasteful!

🤔 What if you only needed 26 packets?

Is it better to buy 3 boxes from Store B (30 packets, $36) or 1 box from Store C (25 packets for $30, then 1 extra from Store A for $1.50)?

3 boxes Store B = $36
Store C + 1 from A = $31.50
It depends…
