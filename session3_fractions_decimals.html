Introduction: Patterns for Parts of a Whole
Patterns aren't just for whole numbers — they work with fractions and decimals too!
Key ideas
🔢
Same rules apply
Add, subtract, multiply, divide — the pattern rules work exactly the same way with decimals and fractions.
🔗
Fractions = Decimals
Counting by ¼ is the same as counting by 0.25. Counting by ½ is the same as counting by 0.5. Two representations, same value.
📈
Two types of patterns
Additive: add or subtract the same amount each step.   Multiplicative: multiply or divide by the same number each step.

Wrap-up: Connecting Rational Patterns
Let's connect what we've learned today!
What is the relationship between counting by 0.25 and counting by ¼?
✅ Exactly right! 0.25 = ¼. They are two ways to write the same number. So counting by 0.25 → 0.25, 0.50, 0.75, 1.00… and counting by ¼ → ¼, ½, ¾, 1… produce identical sequences — just written differently!
