💡 Key Idea

The equals sign means both sides are balanced. To find an unknown, apply the inverse (opposite) operation to both sides — keeping the balance. Today we use this idea with multiplication and division!

⚖️ Introduction · 10 minutes

Balancing Equations

Multiplication and division are inverse operations — they undo each other, just like addition and subtraction.

Multiplication

Division (inverse)

⚖️ The Balance Rule

Whatever we do to one side of an equation, we must do the exact same thing to the other side — to keep it balanced.

To solve 3 × X = 18: divide both sides by 3  →  X = 6
To solve X ÷ 5 = 7: multiply both sides by 5  →  X = 35

📦 Unknown Variable

We use a box □ or letter X to represent the number we don't know yet. Our goal is always to get X alone on one side of the equation.
