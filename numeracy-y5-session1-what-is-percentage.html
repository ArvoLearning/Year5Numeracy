"PER CENT" — WHAT DOES IT MEAN?

Unlocking the meaning behind the % symbol

⏱ 10 mins

💡 The Big Idea

The word "percent" literally means "per hundred" or "out of one hundred." The symbol % is just a shortcut for saying "out of 100"!

25
out of 100
=
25%
twenty-five percent
🪙 Connect it to MONEY: How many cents are in a dollar?
100 cents = $1.00 (the WHOLE dollar)

So if you have 25 cents, you have 25 out of the 100 parts that make a dollar — that's 25% of a dollar!

🏆 100% = THE WHOLE THING

If you get 100% on a test, it means you got every single answer right — the complete, whole test! 100% always means the FULL amount, nothing missing.
