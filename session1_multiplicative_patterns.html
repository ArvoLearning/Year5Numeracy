Key Vocabulary
Multiplier
The number you multiply by to get from one term to the next. The pattern grows.
×2 doubles×3 triples×10
Divisor
The number you divide by to get from one term to the next. The pattern shrinks.
÷2 halves÷3÷10
Successive Term Rule
The rule tells us what to do to get from one term to the next. Finding the rule means you can predict any term in the pattern — not just the next one!
To find the rule: divide any term by the one before it.
e.g. 4, 8, 16 → 8 ÷ 4 = 2, so the rule is ×2
