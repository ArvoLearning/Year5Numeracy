My Money Plan — Budgeting

What is a financial plan and why does it matter?

⏱ 10 mins

A financial plan (or budget) is a plan for how you will use your money — deciding how much comes in, what you'll spend it on, and how much you want to save. It's like a roadmap for your money.

💚

Income

Money that comes in. Could be pocket money, gifts, fundraising, wages.

❤️

Expenses

Money that goes out. Things you buy or pay for — food, tickets, supplies.

💛

Savings

Money left over after expenses. Put aside for future goals or emergencies.

Income − Expenses = Savings (or Shortfall)

🎯 Why is budgeting important?

✅ Achieve goals — saving up for something big becomes possible with a plan
✅ Avoid running out — know before you spend whether you have enough
✅ Make choices — if money is tight, a budget helps you decide what matters most
📐 How we write a budget:
List all income → list all expenses (with calculations) → subtract expenses from income → see if you have a surplus or deficit.
